Pow Pow Pow You're Dead - Game Rules

Objective

The objective is to be the last man standing by "shooting" others with a finger gun and saying "POW POW POW, you're dead" while ensuring they know they have been shot.

Execution

Approach the target and point your finger gun at them. Clearly say "POW POW POW, you're dead."

Awareness

The target must reasonably know they have been shot. This means they should be able to see or hear you. Shooting from a distance or whispering does not count if the target is unaware.

Speed

Say the full phrase quickly. If the target "shoots" back before you finish the phrase, your shot does not count.

Death

Once shot, the target cannot shoot anyone else until the game resets.

Reset

The game resets every hour on the hour, allowing everyone to play again.

Completion

You must aim the finger gun and complete the entire phrase for it to count.

Ongoing

The game can be played at any time, any day, and any place. Strangers can be involved to help "kill" others, but typically you do not involve strangers unless necessary.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I shoot someone while they are sleeping?
A: No, the target must be aware that they have been shot.

Q: Can I use multiple finger guns?
A: Yes, but you must still say the full phrase "POW POW POW, you're dead" for the shot to count.

Q: What if someone claims they didn't hear me?
A: As long as it is reasonable to assume they should have known, the shot counts.
