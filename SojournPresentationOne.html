Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the digital marketing landscape. In this presentation, we will explore some of the key AI tools that Sojourn Digital can leverage to enhance their marketing strategies.

AI Tools Overview

Poe: Customized Bots

Poe is a platform for building customized bots as an alternative to GPTs. The main advantages are the broadening selection of models/features and access to Claude 3.

ChatGPT for SEO Content

Using ChatGPT to identify content holes and produce helpful unique SEO content. The search with Bing feature lets it visit URLs and identify content holes from the top-performing sites on a keyword while double-checking for plagiarism.

Invideo GPT for Video Creation

The Invideo GPT is for video creation. Note: it is pretty bad, but you can edit the videos extensively if necessary.

Ideogram for Free Image Creation

Ideogram is a tool for free image creation, useful for generating unique visuals without additional cost.

Testing GPT-4o for Content Creation

My testing shows that even Claude Haiku is significantly better at style matching from reference material than GPT-4o.
